Muriel Tour

Along the way there will be plenty of opportunities for taking photos and a chance to sign your name on the world famous peace wall that has divided our communities for over 45 years.

What better way to explore the coastal roads of the Giants Causeway than with one of our taxi tours. Our experienced drivers can talk you through the history of this iconic setting, while you can enjoy looking at the beautiful scenery.

Discover the Belfast story, from birth through its turbulent history of conflict and terror to peace and prosperity! Discover points of interest along the way and find out how religion, rebellion, politics, and conflict shaped our cityscape.
